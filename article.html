The Functionality of This Web Tool

How It Works?

This is a simulator that calculates income tax and resident tax for a given year based on input such as annual income and number of dependents using Japan's system.
Calculating tax amounts is by no means straightforward.
To obtain accurate figures, precise calculations are necessary.
Let me explain the calculation method below.

　

Basic Calculation Formula

Total Tax = Income Tax + Resident Tax

Income Tax Calculation Procedure

1. Calculate Annual Income
Income refers to the total amount of earnings for the year, including annual sales for self-employed individuals, and the total yearly salary for employees, part-time, or temporary workers, which may include bonuses.

2. Calculate Employment Income Deductions
This involves calculating the income deductions. First, calculate the employment income deduction, which is allocated equally to everyone. Since the calculation method varies depending on the income, please refer to the table below.

Income 0-1.625M 1.625M-1.8M 1.8M-3.6M 3.6M-6.6 6.6M-8.5M 8.5M~
Formula 550K Income×40% - 100K Income×30% + 80K Income×20% + 440K Income×10% + 1.1M 1.95M

3. Once you calculate employment income deduction, let's subtract it from the income to find the total income.
Total Income = Income - Employment Income Deduction

8. Finally Calculate Total Income Tax
